RaiX — the mascot

RaiX is a female mascot: a red ray-like creature with a double tail. One tail is the classic pointy demon tail (a simple pointed tail), and the other is a venom-style tail with a stripe — a strong visual element rather than a literal mechanic. The red fork you may have noticed in some drawings is a humorous element: RaiX "found a red fork" because she couldn't find a trident — it's a playful touch, not a weapon spec.

RaiX is meant to bring personality and a recognizable identity to the kernel. She represents curiosity, experimentation and a little mischief.

Design notes

  • Color scheme: red accents and dark backgrounds for contrast.
  • Keep mascot illustrations friendly but slightly mischievous.
  • RaiX is not a logo for any other project — she belongs to StingriX.
